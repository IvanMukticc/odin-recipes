Protein Smoothie

Protein Smoothie is perfect breakfast, mix of fruit, oats, mlik, and proteins.

You can drink it on your job, gives you lots of energy.

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop of protein
  • 1 banana
  • 1 scoop of oats
  • 0,2dl of mlik

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients in smoothie maker.
  2. Turn it on.
  3. Enjoy!
