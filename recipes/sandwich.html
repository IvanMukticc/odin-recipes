Egg/Ham Sandwich

Egg sandwich is perfect breakfast, but also can be eaten at every time of a day.

Ingredients

  • Oil and butter
  • 2 or 3 eggs
  • 2 toast bread
  • Ham or any other sandwich meat
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Salt and papper

Steps

  1. Smash 2 eggs into bowl and mix it well,
  2. Put oil and butter on pan, medium fire,
  3. When pan is hot, add eggs and put toast on both sides while eggs are still fluid,
  4. Add salt and papper,
  5. After eggs turn into omlette, switch it on other side,
  6. Add sauce, cheese and ham,
  7. Combine toast bread together,
  8. Enjoy!
